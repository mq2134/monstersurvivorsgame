About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where your goal is simple: stay alive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters. As you progress, you'll earn experience points to level up your character and unlock powerful new abilities and weapons.

With its roguelike elements and procedurally generated content, every playthrough offers a unique experience. Discover different character builds, experiment with various weapon combinations, and develop your own strategies to overcome the monster hordes.

Inspired by popular titles like Vampire Survivors, Monster Survivors adds its own unique twist to the genre with distinctive monster types, special abilities, and power-ups that will keep you engaged for hours on end.

Whether you're looking for a quick gaming session or a long-term challenge to master, Monster Survivors offers accessible gameplay that's easy to learn but difficult to master.

Game Features

Endless Gameplay

Character Progression

Level up your character and unlock new abilities, weapons, and power-ups as you play.

Various Enemy Types

Dynamic Environments

Explore randomly generated maps with different terrains and obstacles to navigate.

Special Abilities

High Score System

How to Play

  • 1.

    Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character around the map.

  • 2.

    Your character will automatically attack nearby monsters.

  • 3.

    Collect experience gems from defeated monsters to level up.

  • 4.

    When you level up, choose from a selection of upgrades to enhance your character.

  • 5.

    Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters.

  • 6.

    Discover different strategies and build combinations to maximize your survival time.
